Privacy Policy for College Biology Ghana


College Biology Ghana is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the practices we follow regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our educational app.

Data Collection

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect any personally identifiable information from you when you use College Biology Ghana. This includes your name, email address, phone number, or any other data that could be used to identify you.

  • Device Information: We may automatically collect certain non-personal information about your device, such as your device type, operating system, and IP address. This information is used to improve the app's performance and compatibility.

Data Usage

  • App Functionality: The information we collect is used solely to ensure the app functions correctly and provides a seamless educational experience.

  • Analytics: We may use analytics tools to gather general information about how users interact with the app. This data is aggregated and does not contain any personally identifiable information.

Data Sharing

  • No Data Sharing: We do not share any information collected from you with third parties.

Security

  • Data Protection: We implement reasonable security measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

Children's Privacy

  • Not Intended for Children: College Biology Ghana is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
